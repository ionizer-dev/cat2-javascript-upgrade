Who We Are

JerseyIT was started in 2025 by Fidel Ramani Macmillan, who got tired of overpriced jerseys and fake kits in Nairobi. What started as buying kits for himself and his friends has grown into a trusted supplier for football fans across Nairobi especially among the youths.

JerseyIT authentic and high-quality replica jerseys for Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and international national teams. Every kit is verified before it is delivered to you.
