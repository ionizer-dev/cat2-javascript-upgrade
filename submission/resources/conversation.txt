 WEB PAGE CLIENT INTERVIEW
Date:        June 2025
Interviewees: Fidel Ramani Macmillan founder JerseyIT
Location:     Nairobi, Kenya

WHO is the owner?
Fidel Ramani MAcmillan is a dedicated  and self-described football
fanatic who co-founded JerseyIT in 2025. Frustrated by the high prices and
poor quality of jerseys available in local shops, he began sourcing authentic
and high-quality replica kits directly and reselling them to friends
and football fans around Nairobi.

WHAT DO THEY DO?
JerseyIT sells football jerseys from top clubs and national teams including
Premier League sides (Arsenal, Chelsea, Man United, Liverpool, Man City),
La Liga clubs (Real Madrid, Barcelona), and African national teams (Kenya,
Nigeria, Senegal, Morocco). They also offer custom name and number printing
and bulk/bundle deals for teams and groups. Orders are delivered countrywide
via courier or collected in person in Nairobi.

TARGET AUDIENCE:
- University students and young football fans in Nairobi
- Supporters looking for verified, quality kits at fair prices
- School and local football teams ordering in bulk

PURPOSE OF THE WEBPAGE:
Macmillan wanted a simple, professional online presence to legitimise
his business, attract new customers who may not know them personally, and
make it easy for people to see what they sell and how to contact  him.

KEY INFORMATION TO DISPLAY:
- Business name and branding
- Their story and who they are
- Product categories and prices
- Gallery of stock  and product variety
- Contact details: phone, email, WhatsApp.

PREFERENCES:
- Visual style: Bold and modern, feels like a real sports brand
- Color scheme: Black background with yellow-green accent, clean white sections
- Tone: Energetic and trustworthy website
- Visitors should be able to reach them immediately via WhatsApp or email

DESIRED ACTION FOR VISITORS:
Contact them on WhatsApp or email to place an order or ask about available stock.
